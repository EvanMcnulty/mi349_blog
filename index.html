The Value of Urban Nature Spaces

June 8, 2025 by Evan McNulty

Urban green spaces are more than just aesthetic additions to a city. They serve as important habitats, provide mental health benefits, and improve air quality. In my experience volunteering in conservation, I’ve seen how transforming even a small vacant lot into a native plant garden can revitalize a community and support pollinators. Cities like Portland and Minneapolis are investing heavily in green infrastructure, creating more walkable and livable neighborhoods.

Lessons From Four Years at Stiles Pond Camp

June 5, 2025 by Evan McNulty

Stiles Pond Day Camp taught me so much—resilience, leadership, patience. Whether it was leading nature hikes or helping kids conquer their fear of the water, I learned the importance of presence and empathy. These experiences made me appreciate the power of outdoor education in shaping confidence and curiosity in kids. Plus, who doesn’t love a lakeside sunset and s'mores?
